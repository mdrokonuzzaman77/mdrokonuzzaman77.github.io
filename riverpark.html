River Park Kitchen

Located in the River Park neighborhood along the Sacramento River, this home is where our client grew up and is now raising her family. The goal of the project was to update and reimagine the galley-style kitchen while respecting the home's character. We added warmth with a neutral color palette and high-quality finishes such as Taj Mahal quartzite on the counters and hand glazed tiles on the backsplash, complemented by brass and black accents. The result is a design that is light and airy all the while paying homage to this 1950's home. Designed | Curated provided cabinetry design, selection of countertops, backsplash, paint colors, and fixtures / finishes.
