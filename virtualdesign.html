Virtual Design

Virtual design is primarily for clients (located near or far) who are seeking a designer’s know-how, but who prefer to implement the design on their own time. It’s ideal for updating one or two rooms in a home, though we’re always open to larger projects. Virtual design includes the option to add on procurement or consultation during the implementation phase of the project.

Virtual Design Consultations

$500 per room

For our virtual design services, we work with clients to come up with a design that includes all of the pieces to complete a beautiful room that meets your needs in a 75-minute virtual meeting. Along with the virtual meeting, this package includes the completion of a mood board with the design created and one revision, if necessary, along with a source list for every piece needed to complete the room & the links on where to purchase.

Virtual Space Planning Consultations

$500 per room

Are you working with a contractor or architect but aren’t sure if the layout or design of your space is right? Our space planning consultation includes a 75-minute virtual meeting to go through the proposed floor plans and discuss in details where things can be improved. This is perfect for focusing on a specific issue that has you feeling a little stuck in the new design of your space (floor plans are needed for this option).

Includes:

  • 75-Minute Consultation
  • Design Recommendation Recap & Notes
